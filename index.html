About

Bridging Engineering & Clinical AI

I am a Biomedical Research Engineer at Synergen Technology Labs and a postgraduate student in Data Science & Artificial Intelligence at the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka. My work sits at the intersection of biosignal processing, clinical AI validation, and regulatory-aligned documentation.

My research interests span auditory attention decoding from EEG using interpretability methods, AI-assisted ECG arrhythmia detection, and retinal vascular biomarker analysis for diabetic screening — all areas where rigorous signal science and trustworthy AI design are equally essential.

I hold a B.Sc. in Biomedical Engineering (Second Class Upper, CGPA 3.57/4.2, Dean's List Semesters 4 & 6) and bring hands-on experience in DFMEA, inter-rater reliability analysis, and regulatory-ready clinical summaries.
